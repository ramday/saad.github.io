Policy Impact on School Enrollment

The Equity Equation Project is transforming education planning in Pakistan by addressing the challenge of 26+ million out-of-school children with advanced computational models.

Learn More

Project Summary

The Equity Equation Project harnesses the power of agent-based modeling, causal graphical modeling, and spatial analysis to simulate policy impacts on school enrollment. Our mission is to empower policymakers to make evidence-based decisions to increase enrollment among marginalized communities.

About the Project

By integrating census data, household surveys, and GIS information, our model offers a comprehensive tool to simulate various policy interventions. This project invites experts in computational modeling, machine learning, and GIS to join us in revolutionizing education planning in Pakistan.

We draw on global research and reports from trusted sources such as the World Bank, UNESCO, and UNICEF to build a robust and scalable model.
